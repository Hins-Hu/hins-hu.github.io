An end-to-end constrained optimization learning framework for Markov decision processes (MDPs) with combinatorially constrained action spaces.

We use a GPT-based sequence model to learn from offline trajectories of on-demand ride-sharing fleets, enabling scalable and efficient decision-making under complex combinatorial constraints.
