A GNN-based supervised learning model that prunes the request-trip-vehicle (RTV) graph used in generating candidate shared trips for high-capacity ride-pooling.

Achieves a **5× speed-up** over hand-crafted heuristics with negligible impact on solution quality, enabling real-time deployment in large-scale ride-sharing systems.
