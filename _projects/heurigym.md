An agentic benchmark evaluating LLM-crafted heuristics on real-world NP-hard combinatorial optimization problems. Introduces the Quality-Yield Index (QYI), a unified metric that captures both solution feasibility and quality.

Key findings reveal that even state-of-the-art models (e.g., Gemini-Pro) achieve less than 60% of expert performance across benchmark tasks.
