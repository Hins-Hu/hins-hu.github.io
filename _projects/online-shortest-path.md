Investigates the reliable online shortest path problem, where reliability accounts for both the mean and standard deviation of travel times.

We develop a provably efficient Lower Confidence Bound (LCB) algorithm within a combinatorial multi-armed bandit framework, with theoretical guarantees on regret bounds.
