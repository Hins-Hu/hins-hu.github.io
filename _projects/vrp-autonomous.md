Formulates a novel vehicle routing problem (VRP) tailored to road networks with limited real-time infrastructure support for autonomous vehicles.

Provides a MILP formulation capturing the unique constraints of semi-autonomous driving, along with a tractable two-phase algorithm that scales to practical problem sizes.
