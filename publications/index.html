  2. ICLR

    HeuriGym: An Agentic Benchmark for LLM-Crafted Heuristics in Combinatorial Optimization
    H. Chen*, Y. Wang*, Y. Cai*, Hins Hu*, and 9 more authors
    The Fourteenth International Conference on Learning Representations, 2026
    * Core contributors
    Abs Paper Bib Code Website

    HeuriGym is an agentic benchmark evaluating LLM-crafted heuristics on real-world NP-hard combinatorial optimization problems. We introduce the Quality-Yield Index (QYI), a unified metric capturing both solution feasibility and quality. Our results reveal that even state-of-the-art models achieve less than 60% of expert performance.
